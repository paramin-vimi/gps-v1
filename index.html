What We Do

Our corporate and commercial law services

Comprehensive support across the full corporate lifecycle — from formation and financing to transactions, governance, and recovery.

01

Commercial Contracts

Every business relationship is ultimately defined by the terms that govern it. We draft, review, and negotiate contracts across procurement, supply, distribution, licensing, and service arrangements, ensuring that obligations are clearly allocated, risk is appropriately managed, and each agreement is capable of being enforced if a dispute should arise.

02

Corporate Governance

Sound governance is the foundation of a well-run and legally compliant company. We advise on board structure, decision-making authority, and internal policy, helping ensure that corporate formalities are properly observed and that management operates within a clear and defensible framework.

03

Debt & Loan Transactions

Financing arrangements carry long-term consequences for both borrower and lender. We structure, document, and negotiate loan agreements and other debt instruments, from term sheets through to execution, so that each party's rights, obligations, and remedies are clearly and enforceably set out.

04

Sales, Mergers & Acquisitions

Corporate transactions involve significant legal, financial, and operational risk. We provide comprehensive support across the transaction lifecycle, including due diligence, deal structuring, negotiation, and completion, working to protect our clients' interests at every stage and see the transaction through to a successful close.

05

Insolvency & Reorganization

Financial distress requires careful navigation of statutory obligations and competing interests. We advise directors, shareholders, and creditors on insolvency, liquidation, and reorganization matters, helping clients understand their legal position and pursue the most viable path forward, whether that means recovery or an orderly wind-down.

06

Compliance & Legal Opinions

Regulatory obligations evolve continuously, and non-compliance can carry significant consequences. We conduct legal and regulatory compliance audits, provide formal legal opinions, and support the ongoing maintenance of corporate records and filings, helping clients remain current with their obligations.

07

Restructuring & Recapitalization

A change in financial circumstances often calls for a change in structure. We advise on the restructuring of debt, equity, and corporate arrangements, working closely with management and stakeholders to reposition the business on a more stable and sustainable footing.

08

Business Entity Formation

The choice of entity and jurisdiction has lasting legal and commercial implications. We assist with the formation of local and foreign business entities, as well as non-profit organizations, advising on structure, governing documents, and regulatory requirements from inception.

09

Strategic Alliances, Joint Ventures & Partnerships

Collaborative ventures can create substantial value, provided the underlying legal arrangements are sound. We advise on and document strategic alliances, joint ventures, and other partnering transactions, addressing governance, contribution, profit allocation, and exit mechanisms with precision.

10

Founder & Shareholder Agreements

The agreements entered into at a company's formation frequently determine how disputes are resolved later on. We draft articles of association, shareholder agreements, and partnership agreements that clearly define ownership, control, and decision-making rights among founders and investors from the outset.
